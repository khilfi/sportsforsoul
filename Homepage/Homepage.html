GAIN REWARDS JUST BY DOING THESE THREE SPORTS

The Prophet (PBUH) emphasized insistently on the games that have a purpose since they are related to the military for men and uttered many hadiths to encourage them. The issues that he dwelled on the most were archery, horse riding and swimming.

In a narration that Tirmidhi states to be "sound", all of the entertainments of man are declared to be wrong except "archery, riding and swimming"; it is stated that they are "appropriate".In a hadith narrated from Umar Ibn Khatab (R.A) in which our beloved Prophet PBUH said, "Teach your children swimming, archery and horse riding".

And in Sahih Muslim, the Prophet PBUH said, "Practice archery and horseback riding."From that point, the Prophet (PBUH) lists the duties of a father toward his children to teach them these sports, which they have great affect the person physically, mentally and spiritually.
